Privacy Policy — ASL Maintenance DB

Last updated: July 2026
Developer Name: dev-himchita  |  Package: com.himadri.asl_maintenance_db

Overview

ASL Maintenance DB is an internal maintenance-management app built for employees of Aarti Steels Limited. It is not distributed to the general public and is intended only for authorised staff to log equipment maintenance records, raise requests, and receive maintenance alerts. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is processed, and where it is stored.

1. Who Can Use This App

Access is restricted to registered employees of Aarti Steels Limited. Employee themselves creates an account before the app can be used. This app is not intended for use by the general public or by children.

2. Information Collected at Registration

When an employee account is created, the following information is collected and stored:

  • Employee ID
  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Designation / department (e.g. Mechanical, E&I)
  • Password — used only to authenticate the account; it is not stored in readable form (see Section 8)

3. Maintenance & Log Data

As part of normal use, the app stores the maintenance and equipment records employees create, including:

  • Equipment/asset entries added to the database
  • Maintenance logs (description, status, department)
  • Photos attached to a database entry or log (see Section 5)
  • GPS coordinates attached to an entry at the time it is created (see Section 6)

This data is used solely to run the maintenance workflow for Aarti Steels Limited and is visible to authorised staff and admin (Central Maintenance) within the organisation.

5. Camera, Photos & Attachments

You can attach a photo (taken with the camera or chosen from your gallery) to an equipment record, log. These photos are uploaded to a private storage bucket on our backend (see Section 7) so that the relevant maintenance team can view them. Photos are not shared outside the organisation.

6. Location Data

When you add a database entry or a maintenance log, the app requests your device's current GPS position once, at that moment, and stores the coordinates alongside the entry. This is used to record where a piece of equipment or an issue is physically located on-site. The app does not track your location continuously or in the background.

7. Backend & Data Storage — Appwrite

The app stores accounts, maintenance records, and photo attachments using Appwrite Cloud (Singapore region), a backend-as-a-service platform:

sgp.cloud.appwrite.io

  • Account data (Section 2) is stored via Appwrite Authentication.
  • Maintenance/log/database records (Section 3) are stored via Appwrite Databases.
  • Photo attachments (Section 5) are stored via Appwrite Storage, in a private bucket accessible only to authenticated app users.

Appwrite acts as our data processor. Data stored here is only accessible to authenticated employees of Aarti Steels Limited through the app, and to administrators managing the backend.

8. Authentication & Password Storage

Passwords are handled by Appwrite's authentication service and are never stored or visible in plain text to the app or its developer. Session tokens are stored securely on your device to keep you signed in between app launches.

9. Push Notifications

The app uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google LLC) to deliver maintenance alerts. A device push token is registered with Firebase so the app can receive these alerts; this token is not used for advertising or shared with any advertising network.

10. Exporting & Sharing Reports

The app can generate PDF and CSV reports from maintenance data. When you choose to export or share a report, it is handed to your device's standard OS share sheet (e.g. email, WhatsApp, Google Drive) — the app itself does not upload these exports anywhere beyond what you explicitly choose to share.

11. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No advertising identifiers, and the app shows no ads
  • No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs are integrated
  • No contacts, call logs, browsing history, or clipboard data
  • No continuous or background location tracking

13. Data Retention & Deletion

Account data as well as equipment and maintenance data is retained for as long as required for Aarti Steels Limited's operational and record-keeping needs. To request deletion of your account or associated data CLICK HERE or contact the Central Maintenance department at aarti.maintenance.db@gmail.com. Requests will be handled within 30 days.

14. Children's Privacy

This app is intended solely for adult employees of Aarti Steels Limited and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18 through this app.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app's features change. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any revisions.

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact: aarti.maintenance.db@gmail.com
